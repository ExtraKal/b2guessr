How to Play

1. Start a game and look at the image on the left.

2. Pick the map you think the image came from.

3. On the full map, click where the center of the image should be, then press CONFIRM GUESS.

4. If you picked the wrong map, you get 0 for that round.

5. If you picked the right map, you can earn up to 100 points based on how close your click is to the true center.

Zoom: use the zoom controls or your mouse wheel, then drag the map to pan around.

Modifiers: black and white, inverted colors, pixelated, and upside down only affect the image.

Timer: each run tracks your total time, with separate personal bests for each ruleset.

Difficulties: Beginner shows the largest crop. CHIMPS is the most zoomed in.
